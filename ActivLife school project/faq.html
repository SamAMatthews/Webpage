Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is this website so janky?

Good question! This entire webpage is currently being held together solely by a mix of code from my existing website, stuff from various web dev forums, most of which I don't understand, and enough dark internet magic to kill a small town of real web developers. In all honesty, I do not understand a single line of the JavaScript program that this site uses to hide the navbar when you scroll down. I don't know how it works at all. Heck, it could be summoning some little green goblin from deep within the W3Schools webservers for all I know- I don't have a clue! In fact, I pray to the internet gods every night that this dumpster fire of a website remains functional when I wake up the next day! At one point, I even considered making a blood sacrifice to The Great CSSus (the god of CSS) when I was struggling to get this content box to work, but luckily it started working as I had hoped before that became necessary.

TL;DR, this site is held together almost exclusively by wishful thinking, and oh boy does it show.
